About us

Star Cinema Club is a nonprofit home for independent, international, and local film. As the only nonprofit cinema in El Paso city, we are committed to enhancing the cultural vitality of the community through quality film and educational programming.


Star Cinema Club has a rich history established by a small band of community organizers and made possible by many dedicated volunteers who have shaped our history and helped the cinema thrive since its inception as a non-profit nearly four years ago.


Star Cinema opened as a nonprofit in 2016 with only two paid staff and a group of volunteers. Since that humble start, we have continued to add quality programming, film festivals, filmmaker outreach, community events, education programs, and talented staff and volunteers to our ranks.
